Chain Pop — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

Chain Pop ("we", "the app") is a free puzzle game for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All game progress (level unlocks, star ratings, settings) is stored locally on your device only using an on-device database (Hive). No accounts are required. No data is synced to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate location (IP-based, not GPS)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and UK, a consent form is shown before ads are personalized, in accordance with GDPR requirements.

Children's Privacy

Chain Pop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. The app's ad configuration does not request child-directed treatment.

Data Retention & Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the in-app "Reset all progress" option
  • Clearing the app's data in Android Settings
  • Uninstalling the app

To reset your advertising identifier, visit your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
